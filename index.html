Welcome to my site, where I have presented some of the best things I have drawn.
I have only fairly recently become an artist, but I am already hooked by the freedom it creates
I am 17, and I have lived in Devon all my life only moving from Kingsteignton to Newton Abbot.
I am in full-time education at Newton Abbot College, hoping to go to Bristol University to study Computer Science.
I built this whole site with HTML, using BootStrap as a starting point for my Style Sheets.
I was inspired to start exploring art by a youtuber called Nevercake. His simplistic drawing and animation styles appealed to me, and I decided to try to emulate him.
From here, I have tried a range of different art styles, with different levels of success.
The inspiration for individual pieces is normally other works I have found on the internet, but I have created a few originals as well.
In the future, I plan to expand the different art styles i have tried, maybe trying some different types of landscapes, as I normally only do portraits.
I am also starting to explore photo-realism styles.
